Frequently Asked Questions


Q. If I rent the trailer for multiple days, when & how can the bathroom be serviced?

A. The rental bathroom will be serviced as often as you like and you will be charged $100.00 extra dollars after the first cleaning. Just call us if you need it serviced.


Q. How much would it cost for an attendant for an 8 hr. event?

A. The cost would be $200.00 dollars per 8 hrs. The attendant will maintain the bathroom during the event and make sure everything is clean. You can see the prices of all of our additional servies here.


Q. How much would it cost to have live flowers placed in the bathroom area?

A. The cost would would be $100.00 dollars for live flowers of your choice. You can see the prices of all of our additional servies here.


Q. What happens if something is broken or destroyed in your bathroom?

A. You will sign an additionally insured clause in your contract that will cover any damages.


Q. How do I insure that the toilet smells fresh and clean throughout the events?

A. In your price quote you will be provided with a bio-degradable refresher that can be placed in the toilet.


Q. What does each type of trailer come equipped with?
A. All trailers are described under the rentals page. Also, check under the Additional Services listed there for anything you would like in addition to your trailer.
